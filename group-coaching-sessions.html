Frequently Asked Questions

What is link building?
Link building is a process of acquiring backlinks from other websites to a website with the purpose of increasing its visibility in search engine results.
How do I create high-quality backlinks?
High quality backlinks can be created by creating valuable content, guest blogging on authoritative websites, broken link building, directory submission and using influencers to share your content.
What techniques should I avoid when doing link building?
Techniques such as buying links or participating in link farms should be avoided as they are considered unethical and will likely result in search engine penalties.
Is there any risk involved in link building?
Yes, there is always a risk involved in link building as it can lead to unnatural linking patterns which could result in search engine penalties if done incorrectly.
What metrics are used to measure the success of a link-building campaign?
Metrics such as domain authority, page authority, referring domains and anchor text diversity are used to measure the success of a link-building campaign.
